Enterprise-Grade Architecture for Home Use

Keep unauthorized devices locked out entirely. KidsAccount enforces rigorous, decentralized safety safeguards:

🛡️

Device Whitelisting Gateway

New devices calculate a hardware fingerprint on first visit. They remain completely unauthorized until an active system Admin manually flags approval.

🔑

Secure Admin Password Checkpoints

Switching profiles to administrative accounts triggers localized, password-verified runtime gates ensuring kids can't manipulate their own balance sheets.
